12-day Gaming Tournament held

CGL Esports, an organisation run by youths, organised a 12-day Gaming Tournament on Canadian University of Bangladesh (CUB) campus in the capital that ended on December 30, said a press release. A total of 12 teams participated and among them, the best eight teams got the chance to play the CSGO LAN tournament. Professional players from all around Bangladesh participated in the gaming contest to become the winner of the title ‘CGL CSGO MASTERS LAN’.
